Goal is to improve the user interface for easier navigation, better filtering for services, and enhanced visibility of fuel prices.

Project Overview

This project, undertaken as part of my UX/UI Design journey, focuses on the redesign of Z Energy’s station locator and fuel price information pages. Z Energy, a leading fuel retailer in New Zealand, caters to a diverse range of customers, including road trip enthusiasts and everyday commuters, seeking reliable and efficient fuel services. With the growing need for accessible and intuitive digital tools in the energy sector, this project aims to modernize Z Energy's platform to address key user challenges. By improving navigation, enhancing service filtering options, and increasing the visibility of fuel prices, the redesign creates a user-centered experience that not only simplifies trip planning but also reinforces Z Energy’s reputation for customer-focused innovation.

Solution

  • Intuitive Navigation and User-Centered Design: Redesign the station locator and fuel price pages to provide a seamless browsing experience. Implement an improved layout with clear navigation, enabling users to effortlessly find nearby stations, services, and fuel prices.

  • Real-Time Fuel Pricing and Service Information: Introduce a visually clear and dynamic fuel price display, updated in real-time, to help users make informed decisions. Enhance service details with easy-to-read icons and descriptions, making it straightforward to identify key offerings at each station, such as EV charging or car washes.

  • Responsive and Accessible Design: Create a mobile-first, fully responsive platform that works seamlessly across all devices, ensuring accessibility for all users, including those on the go. Adhere to modern accessibility standards to accommodate diverse user needs, delivering a design that is inclusive, visually appealing, and highly functional.

  • Trip Planning Made Easy:ntegrate a route-planning feature that allows users to locate stations along their journey, complete with fuel price comparisons and service availability. This user-friendly tool ensures road trip enthusiasts and commuters alike can optimize their routes and manage costs effectively.

Z Energy Usability Study

The primary objective of this study was to evaluate the usability of the redesigned Z Energy station locator and Journey Planner pages using Jakob Nielsen’s 10 usability heuristics. The goal was to ensure the design solutions met users' needs related to navigation, filtering, and information visibility, thus enhancing the overall user experience.

Methodologies for testing

Heuristic Evaluation: Pre-identified potential usability issues using a team-based heuristic evaluation workshop.

Moderated Usability Testing: Conducted task-based testing to assess each heuristic.

Participants: A mix of road trip planners, frequent travelers, and users familiar with digital fuel station locators.

Metrics Tracked: Task completion rate, error rate, task completion time, and satisfaction score (Likert scale).

Key Findings in testing

Overall Usability Satisfaction: Users rated the overall usability of the redesign positively, with scores generally between 4 and 5.

Common Suggestions:

  • Include clear labels or tooltips for icons.
  • Add fuel prices to maps and information cards.
  • Remove or reposition redundant back buttons.

Conclusions for testing

The redesigned Z Energy station locator and fuel price information pages generally performed well against Nielsen’s 10 usability heuristics. Key strengths included effective visibility of system status, user control, and consistency. Areas for improvement focused on clearer iconography, enhanced map details, and refining certain UI elements like the back button.

Recommendations for Next Steps to develop High Fidelity Wireframe

  • Update service icons and add labels to make them more recognizable.
  • Display fuel prices on the map and within information cards.
  • Reposition or remove confusing UI elements, such as the back button in the filter section.

Highlights of the Project

Key Highlights

  • Real-Time Fuel Pricing: Enhanced transparency with prominently displayed, real-time fuel prices for informed decision-making.
